About

My name is Will Pierce. I work as a technical writer and researcher, especially in the area information security.

I’ve contributed documentation and code to open source projects including the Rust compiler, SecureDrop, Qubes OS, OpenOversight, and Flux.

Before working in technology, I did doctoral research in English literature at Northwestern University, working on a dissertation about surveillance and theology in Elizabethan England.
